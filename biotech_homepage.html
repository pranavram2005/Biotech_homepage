About Us

Thinking outside the Petri dish

The Biotech Club, born in 2023 as a vertical under iGEM, is a group of passionate individuals determined to showcase the wonders of biotech to the student community. Our vision is to create an inclusive interdisciplinary haven where students can explore exciting ideas and endless possibilities in biotechnology. The club organizes a series of captivating events and engaging workshops to stimulate your brain, facing those ideas that keep you up at night (zombie apocalypse, anyone?). Apart from this, the club undertakes student projects in biotechnology. At the Biotech Club, the only prerequisite is a deep-seated desire to dive into biotechnology. The club offers a haven for anyone looking to get their hands dirty, ideate and dig deep to come up with solutions. Together we will turn dreams into reality, one DNA strand at a time!
